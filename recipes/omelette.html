Omelette Recipe

Description

This recipe will help you make Omelettes.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 pack of spam
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • Cheese (however much you want)

Steps

  1. Slice spam, bell peppers, and onions into cubes
  2. Cook eggs and add in the sliced ingredients with cheese
